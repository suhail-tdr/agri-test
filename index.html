FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can I set up Agri Cloud on my farm?+
Most farmers are fully set up within 48 hours. Our team remotely guides you through sensor placement and app configuration. You don't need any technical background — just a smartphone.
Does it work in areas with poor mobile connectivity?+
Yes. Our IoT sensors use LoRaWAN — a low-power, long-range radio network that works even with weak 2G coverage. Data is synced to the cloud whenever connectivity is available and cached locally.
What crops does the AI support?+
Our AI model supports 80+ crops common in South and Central India, including rice, coconut, banana, sugarcane, cotton, turmeric, pepper, rubber, and all major vegetables. New crops are added every quarter.
Is my farm data secure and private?+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are ISO 27001 certified. Your farm data is never sold or shared with third parties. You own your data — you can export or delete it at any time.
How much does Agri Cloud cost?+
We offer a free 30-day trial with full features. After that, plans start at ₹499/month for up to 5 hectares. Pro and Enterprise plans are available for larger farms. Hardware (sensors) is a one-time purchase with 2-year warranty.
